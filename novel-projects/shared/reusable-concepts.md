# Reusable Concepts

Generic world fragments and plot devices that can be adapted.

---

## Power Systems

### Concept A: Sound-Based Powers
- User identifies/anipulates through sound frequencies
- Limitation: requires silence or specific acoustic environment
- Upgrade path: amplify, distort, echo, cancel

### Concept B: Neural Link
- Two minds connect, share senses/traits
- Limitation: emotional bleed-through, dependency
- Risk: overload, psychosis if disconnected abruptly

### Concept C: Digital Manifestation
- Virtual objects become real within a bounded digital space
- Limitation: only works in specific locations (labs, servers)
- Rule: what happens digitally can affect physically
