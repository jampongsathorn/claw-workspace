# Shared Themes

Recurring themes and emotional beats across novels.

---

## Core Themes

| Theme | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Identity & Mask** | Characters hiding their true selves |
| **Forbidden Connection** | Love that shouldn't happen |
| **Sacrifice** | Giving up something for someone else |
| **Memory & Loss** | Forgetting or being forgotten |
| **Redemption** | A second chance one doesn't deserve |

---

## Emotional Beats Library

- First meeting (hate at first sight / intrigue)
- Forced proximity → growing attachment
- Secret revealed → conflict
- Confession under pressure
- Sacrifice moment
- Reunion after separation
- Final choice / climax decision
