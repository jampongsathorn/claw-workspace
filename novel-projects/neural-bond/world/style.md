# Neural Bond — Writing Style & Tone

## 📝 Writing Style

| Attribute | Setting |
|-----------|---------|
| **Overall Tone** | Emotional slow-burn, sci-fi atmosphere with intimate romance |
| **POV** | Third person limited (alternating between ใหม่ and เอ็กซ์) |
| **Pacing** | Slow build in early episodes → intense in mid → emotional climax in late |
| **Setting** | Contemporary Thai university + near-future tech lab |

---

## 🎬 Prose Style

- **Description:** Cinematic, sensory-heavy (especially sound)
- **Dialogue:** Casual Thai university speech patterns, mix of คุณ and informal
- **Internal Monologue:** Present for main characters, sparse for side characters
- **Action Scenes:** Short punchy sentences, high tension
- **Romance Scenes:** Slower pacing, detailed sensory focus

---

## 🗣 Dialogue Style

- **ใหม่:** Bright, informal, uses ๆ a lot, talks more than he should
- **เอ็กซ์:** Minimal words, cold, direct, only speaks when necessary
- **Side characters:** Natural Thai banter, mix of friend groups

---

## 🎭 POV Rules

- **Episode structure:** Rotate POV every chapter (or every scene break)
- **POV marker:** Use `===` divider between POV shifts
- **Inner thoughts:** Italics for thoughts, plain for dialogue
- **Sound focus:** Always emphasize what characters HEAR (core theme)

---

## 🚫 Do NOT

- Rush the romance — earn every confession
- Break the ice-cold personality of X
- Use modern slang or anachronistic references
- Describe technology in technobabble — keep it visual and intuitive
- Give ใหม่ a "damsel" role — he's stubborn and capable

---

## ✅ DO

- Use sound as a narrative device (frequencies, echoes, silence)
- Show vulnerability through small moments, not big speeches
- Build tension through withheld information (not action)
- Make the AI feel like a character, not just a tool
- Reference Thai university culture naturally
