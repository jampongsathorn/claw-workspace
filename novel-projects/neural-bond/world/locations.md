# Neural Bond — Locations

## 🏛️ Main University Campus

**Name:** มหาวิทยาลัยเทคโนโลยีพระจอมเกล้า (KMUTT — fictionalized version)

### Building B4 — Old Engineering Building
**Type:** Abandoned research wing (officially condemned)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Floors | B1 (server room), B2-B4 (labs) |
| Access | Voice-activated hidden stairwell behind maintenance closet, 3rd floor |
| Atmosphere | Dim emergency lights, dust, humming servers, cold |
| Secret | เอ็กซ์'s hidden AI lab — "Project EVE" |

**Why it's abandoned:** Officially condemned for structural issues. Reality: a professor covered up an accident here 10 years ago. เอ็กซ์ discovered the space and repurposed it secretly.

---

## 🧪 Key Locations

### B4-3 — เอ็กซ์'s Secret Lab
**Description:** Small space packed with equipment. Multiple monitors showing waveforms. Server rack humming in corner. Blue LED strips on ceiling. Old mattress in corner (he sleeps here more than his dorm).

**Key Items:**
- Main terminal (runs EVE AI)
- Neural earpiece array (6 custom-built units)
- Sound-dampening booth (for high-focus diagnosis)
- Whiteboard covered in equations

---

### B4-1 — Server Room
**Description:** Rows of black server racks, constant cooling hum. Temperature drops 10 degrees from entrance. Blue-lit darkness.

**Significance:** Core processing for EVE. This is where เอ็กซ์'s AI "lives."

---

### ห้องเซิร์ฟเวอร์ — Shared Computer Lab (Public)
**Type:** Normal university computer lab, 2nd floor Engineering building

**When used:** Where เอ็กซ์ does public-facing work (teaching, demos) to maintain cover as normal student.

---

### ห้องพยาบาล — Hospital Training Center
**Type:** Simulated hospital ward for medical students

**Description:** Realistic mock hospital rooms, equipment identical to real hospitals. Smell of antiseptic. Fluorescent lighting.

**When used:** ใหม่'s clerkship location. Where เอ็กซ์ brings "patients" (really test cases) for AI diagnosis.

---

### ห้องเรียน — University Classroom
**Type:** Large lecture hall, tiered seating

**When used:** First meeting scene. ใหม่ sees เอ็กซ์ for first time — but only his back/shoulder in crowd.

---

### ห้องสมุด — University Library, 4th Floor
**Type:** Quiet study area, individual carrels

**Atmosphere:** Dead silence except for turning pages and occasional cough.

**When used:** ใหม่ studies here. First real one-on-one conversation with เอ็กซ์ (he finds her here to recruit).

---

### ลานกลางคืน — Open Courtyard at Night
**Type:** University plaza between buildings

**Atmosphere:** Empty at 2AM. Warm yellow lamp posts. Distant city sounds. Secret meeting spot.

**When used:** Emotional conversations. First time they talk outside the lab.

---

## 🏠 Character Living Spaces

### ใหม่'s Dorm Room
**Type:** Small shared dorm, 2 people per room

**Vibe:** Messy desk, textbooks stacked, fairy lights on wall, small desk fan. Warm and lived-in.

---

### เอ็กซ์'s Apartment (Rarely Seen)
**Type:** Small studio near campus

**Vibe:** Minimalist, all black furniture, single window, no decoration except server equipment. Feels like a place to exist, not live.

---

## 🌍 Outside Campus

### ตลาดกลางคืน — Night Market
**Type:** Popular street food market

**Vibe:** Loud, colorful, crowded, smells of grill smoke and jasmine. Chaotic energy.

**When used:** Bonding scene — ใหม่ takes เอ็กซ์ to experience "normal life" (he hates crowds but she makes him go).

---

### ห้องปฏิบัติการ — Hospital (Real)
**Type:** Actual teaching hospital

**When used:** In later episodes when cases become real patients, not just test scenarios.
