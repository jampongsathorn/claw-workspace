# Neural Bond — Power System

## 🤝 Neural Bond (Core Mechanic)

**What it is:** A two-person audio link where one person hears for the other.

### How It Works
- เอ็กซ์ wears a neural earpiece connected to ใหม่'s hearing
- Audio from ใหม่'s surroundings streams directly to เอ็กซ์'s neural input
- เอ็กซ์'s AI processes the audio and interprets patterns
- เอ็กซ์ tells ใหม่ what to do ("Patient has arrhythmia — check pulse")

### Bond Requirements
- Physical proximity (within 10 meters)
- Mutual focus/attention
- Emotional stability on ใหม่'s part (panic = static)

### Bond Side Effects
- **Emotional bleed-through:** ใหม่ can faintly feel เอ็กซ์'s emotions
- **Sound memory:** เอ็กซ์ sometimes hears echoes of past sounds ใหม่ experienced
- **Dependency:** Extended use makes disconnection physically disorienting

---

## 🎧 AI Sound Diagnosis (เอ็กซ์'s Core Ability)

### Process
1. Raw audio enters via ใหม่'s ears
2. AI parses frequency patterns, tone variations, micro-tremors
3. Matches against disease signature database
4. Outputs: likely condition + confidence %

### Limitations
- Works only with live sound (no recordings)
- Requires near-silence environment (noise = interference)
- Cannot detect mental illness (no reliable audio signature)
- AI confidence drops below 70% for rare diseases

### AI Personality
- Called "EVE" (Evolutionary Voice Engine)
- Speaks in calm female voice
- Has subtle dry humor
- Treats เอ็กซ์ like a child she worries about

## ⚡ Hearing Loss (เอ็กซ์'s Condition)

### Progression
| Stage | Timeline | Effect |
|-------|----------|--------|
| Stage 1 | Months 1-2 | Mild tinnitus, occasional static |
| Stage 2 | Months 3-4 | Difficulty with high frequencies |
| Stage 3 | Month 5 | Narrowing audible range |
| Stage 4 | Month 6 | Complete deafness |

### Symptoms
- Tinnitus (ringing that isn't there)
- Hyperacusis (certain frequencies cause pain)
- Sound distortion (voices sound "muffled" even when loud)
- Eventually: total silence

### Why It Happens
_(classified — revealed in mid-story)_
- Years of high-frequency sound exposure for AI training
- Genetic predisposition to auditory nerve degradation
- Accelerated by stress and exhaustion

## 🩺 ใหม่'s Hearing Ability

### What Makes It Special
- Can hear frequencies beyond normal human range (up to 25kHz)
- Can identify emotional state from voice micro-variations
- Exceptional at distinguishing overlapping sounds

### Medical Application
- Can hear heart murmurs that even machines miss
- Can detect irregular breathing patterns
- Can identify fear/trauma from voice tremor alone

### Limitation
- No pathological knowledge without training → depends on เอ็กซ์'s interpretation

## 🧪 Anti-Neural Bond (Antagonist's Version)

### What It Is
A weaponized version of the neural link that forces connection rather than allowing voluntary bond.

### Danger
- One-way connection (victim cannot disconnect)
- Used to extract information or cause sensory overload
- Creates permanent hearing damage after extended use
