## 📝 Prompt Writing Rules

- Always start with: age, ethnicity, gender
- Build: hair → eyes → face → body → outfit → lighting → vibe
- Quality tags: `, highly detailed, cinematic lighting, 8k, sharp focus`
- X's vibe is COLD + MYSTERIOUS + INTELLIGENT
- Use blue/purple/cool lighting to emphasize isolation
- Negative: `, warm expression, friendly, smiling, cheerful, blurry`
